About Me

Hello, I'm Cher, just a regular gal from El Centro, California, born as Cherilyn Sarkisian on May 20, 1946.
My life's been quite the journey, filled with ups and downs. In the swinging '60s, I kicked off my career with Sonny & Cher, singing hits like "I Got You Babe."
But it wasn't all glitz and glam; I faced my share of struggles along the way.
Despite the challenges, music has always been my passion, thanks to my mom, Georgia Holt, who passed on her love for performing.
And when I'm not belting out tunes, you'll find me fighting for causes close to my heart, like supporting veterans and kids in need.
My love life's been a rollercoaster too, with marriages and breakups making headlines. But through it all, I've stayed true to myself and kept on singing.
Life's been one heck of a ride, but I wouldn't trade it for the world.

Three Main Differences Between Web1, Web2, and Web3

  1. Web1: The first version of the web, primarily accessed through simple text-based browsers and based on static text content.
  2. Web2: The evolution of the web into interactive platforms, allowing for higher interactivity, user-generated content, and dynamic content creation.
  3. Web3: The future development of the web based on blockchain technologies and artificial intelligence, featuring frictionless payment websites, smart contracts, and decentralized applications.

Explanation with Usage Scenario Examples:

Web1:

When searching for drug side effects on Web1, you may come across static pages with basic and often outdated information, without the ability to influence or contribute content.

Web2:

A search for the same drug side effects on Web2 might lead you to forums, drug review websites, or Facebook groups where users share their experiences and answer similar questions. Here, you can receive close and personal information from other users, ask questions, and contribute your insights.

Web3:

In Web3, this search could be conducted through blockchain-based platforms where you can access smart contracts based on user data. Instead of regular websites, you might use decentralized applications that allow you to securely search for information and have an advantage over regular users.
